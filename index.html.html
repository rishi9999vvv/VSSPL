About Us

Vibrant Shipping Services Private Limited is your trusted logistics partner based in Ludhiana, delivering customized freight solutions with precision and care.

We specialize in freight forwarding, customs clearance, warehousing, and multimodal transport across India and abroad. Our focus lies in enabling seamless trade through efficient documentation, proactive communication, and end-to-end shipment tracking. With a growing international network, we cater to clients of all sizes, ensuring timely and cost-effective logistics every step of the way.

Meet Our Founder

Mr. Vijay Dwivedi, Founder and Managing Director of Vibrant Shipping Services, brings over two decades of experience in global logistics and supply chain operations. His leadership has shaped the company’s commitment to excellence and innovation, driving reliable logistics outcomes for diverse industries worldwide.
